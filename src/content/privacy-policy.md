## Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

InvoiceRank ("we", "us", "our") operates the invoicerank.com website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our site.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. However, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

**Google Analytics (GA4)** tracks anonymous usage data including pages visited, time on site, device type, and general location. This helps us understand which content is useful and improve the site. Google Analytics uses cookies. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

**Google AdSense** displays advertisements on our site. AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

## Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device. Our site uses cookies through:

- Google Analytics (traffic analysis)
- Google AdSense (ad personalization)

Most browsers let you refuse cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent. Note that some parts of the site may not function properly without cookies.

## Data Retention

We do not store personal data. Analytics data is retained by Google according to their data retention policies.

## Children
